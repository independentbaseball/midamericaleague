This is the Web3 website for the Mid America League

The Mid America League is a professional baseball development league with 6 teams in the United States. In addition to the regular business model of providing local family-friendly entertainment, the League has decided to pursue new Web3 actions to grow our fan base across North America and elsewhere around the world. One of the ways will be to use Web3 technology such as NFTs, virtual lands (aka digital real estate) in metaverses, POAPs, and other Web 3.0 content. We look forward to growing our league with you so that whenever you think about professional baseball then you will have a favorable name at the top of your list: the Mid America League!

The First Web3 Website On The .probaseball Domain Launched By An Actual Team Or League

As of the first week of November 2023, this is the first version of our web3 website. Additionally, it is the first web3-oriented website ever launched by a professional baseball team or league, You can find this website on web3-enabled browsers by visiting https://midamericaleague.probaseball We also aim to make this content able to be read by regular (web 2.0) browsers such as Chrome, Safari, Firefox and others without requiring any sort of browser plugin or extension. If you already understand web3 domains and would like to see if your preferred domain is available then just click the button link below to see current availability. Web3 domains offer more than just decentralized websites like this one. They offer you an easy-to-remember name to send/receive crypto payments to your wallet plus other benefits such as: * Own your digital identity with more control in web3 * Decentralized email and web conferencing options * No annual domain renewals like you would need to pay with a .com or traditional domain * Much more

Metaverses And Virtual Worlds

At the time of this website's launching (November 2023), no professional baseball team or league yet has figured out a tailored approach to using virtual worlds (metaverses) to help their fans worldwide have a better overall experience. So we are open to your suggestions! Over time we may run games and contests on some or all of these digital real estate platforms, some of which require downloading access to that platform's metaverse or corresponding smart phone app. Others do not require any such download nor any equipment such as Virtual Reality headsets or Augmented Reality glasses. The following links will take you to the corresponding virtual lands for the first stadium announced for the League: Veterans Field in Sherman, Texas. Over time on this website and our social media, we will announce any specific contests or incentives to interactive with the Sherman team and/or the Mid America League in a specific virtual world. Each metaverse virtual world platform has different features it can offer a baseball fan like you, so in the future we will clarify why you might wish to visit one or more of these virtual worlds.

Pocket Schedule NFTs

A little known aspect of working in the minor leagues of professional baseball is the constant requests for official pocket schedules. "Sked" collectors are a rabid subset of the sports memorabilia industry and they have various websites and online groups dedicated just to schedule collecting: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sked The Mid America League plans to be the first league to issue a limited number of official pocket schedules in NFT format on an established NFT platform such as OpenSea.io. POAPs Of Our Games

Some NFT fans also like Proof-of-Attendance Protocol (POAP) digital assets. These are like digital ticket stubs from a game, except they are verified on the blockchain to confirm ownership. The Mid America League plans to issue POAPs, and we are refining our processes to make sure that they bring you benefit possibly beyond just confirming your attendance at one of our games. We may incorporate discounts from sponsors or other benefits because we want to thank you for spending your hard-earned money and time at one of our games or other events! More information on POAPs coming soon.

Web3 In The Future

Since the Mid America League is launching its inaugural season in 2024, much of the league's time and budget has to go to establishing the main business model of selling tickets and running quality baseball games in our local markets. Therefore the following web3 topics may be implemented, but likely not until way after the first season concludes: * A league-focused crypto token * Web3 / crypto-based baseball games, even if just on a phone app * DAOs such as a community which then can vote on player of the year * Fan avatars with digital wearables from their favorite Mid America League teams * Digitally-verified autographs from your favorite players on NFT cards As the league announces any steps forward on these topics then we will announce them on this website, the league's main website (MidAmericaLeague.com), and our social media properties.
